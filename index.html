First approximation to Dhanbad roads project

This preliminary report compiles observations gathered by the team during a series of site visits surrounding. Phase One of the Dhanbad roads project. All initial analysis and remarks will focus on a 400-meter segment of Road 13 located between Birsa Munda Chowk and Joraphatak Chowk (J13-4). Road 13 displays a diverse array of land-uses and users along the streets as they are shared by pedestrians, cars, rickshaws, auto-rickshaws, trucks and street vendors.
